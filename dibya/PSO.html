Program Specific Outcome (PSOs)


CIVIL ENGINEERING


  • By the completion of Civil Engineering program the students will be able to design and build civil engineering based systems in the context of environmental, economical, and societal requirements
  • By the completion of Civil Engineering program the students will be able to identify research gaps and hence provide innovative solutions to Civil engineering problems and engage in life-long learning for professional growth
  • By the completion of Civil Engineering program the students will be able to serve the community as ethical and responsible professionals

COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING


  • The ability to understand and develop computer programs in the areas related to business intelligence, big data analytics, web design and networking for efficienct design of computer-based system of varying complexity.
  • The ability to apply standard practices and strategy in software project development using open ended programming environments to deliver a quality product for business success.

COMPUTER SCIENCE AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


  • The ability to understand and develop computer programs in the areas related to business intelligence, big data analytics, web design and networking for efficient design of computer-based system of varying complexity
  • The ability to apply standard practices and strategy in software project development using open ended programming environments to deliver a quality product for business success.

ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING


  • Modelling and Analysis – An ability to mathematically model and analyse the performance of Electrical Machines, Control systems, Instrumentation systems, Power systems and Power Electronic systems.
  • Design and Development – An ability to design the hardware and software requirements for the development of Electric drives Automation and Embedded systems.
  • Ability to apply mathematical methodologies to solve computation task, model real world problem using appropriate data structure and suitable algorithm.

ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING


  • Identify, Formulate, design prototype and test modern analog and digital electronic systems.
  • Architect, partition and select appropriate technologies for electronic devices and wired/wireless communication system function effectively.
  • Carryout research in niche areas of electronics and communication engineering.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING


  • Graduates of Mechanical Engineering will achieve excellence in product design, thermal engineering and manufacturing system, innovation and entrepreneurship by acquiring knowledge in mathematics, science and designing principles.
  • Graduates will be able to design an experiment as well as to analyse, interpret and provide solutions to the real-life mechanical engineering problems.
  • Graduates will be able to understand the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental, and societal context and to use the principles in the multidisciplinary problem.
